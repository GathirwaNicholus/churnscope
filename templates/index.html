What is this?

ChurnScope is a machine-learning pipeline that predicts which telecom customers are likely to cancel their subscription. It uses a Random Forest classifier trained on customer demographics, service usage, and billing data.

The goal: give retention teams an early-warning signal so they can intervene before a customer churns.

How to use

  1. 01Go to Analytics to explore the dataset and model performance charts.
  2. 02Go to Predict to enter a single customer's details and get a real-time churn probability.
  3. 03Use the /api/predict endpoint to integrate predictions into your own system.

Dataset

Synthetic Telco Customer Churn dataset ({{ "{:,}".format(summary.total_customers) }} rows), modelled after the IBM Telco Churn benchmark. Features include contract type, internet service, tenure, billing, and add-on services.
